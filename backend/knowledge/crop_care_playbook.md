# Crop Care Playbook

## Tomato
- High risk period: warm and humid conditions.
- Watch for foliar fungal diseases after repeated rainfall.
- Maintain balanced nitrogen to avoid dense canopies.

## Rice
- Standing water and warm humidity increase disease pressure.
- Monitor early tillering and reproductive stages closely.

## Wheat
- Risk increases with prolonged dew and moderate temperatures.
- Avoid excess irrigation near heading stage.
